Edutrip Jogja - Solo

For the first 4 days in Jogja we stayed at village Pentingsari. During those days we participated in activities the villagers there usually do. On the 5th day we packed our luggage and said goodbye to the families we stayed with, our stops were museum Ullen Sentallu, museum Gunung Api Merapi, Candi prambanan, Sendratari Ramayana and after that we finally checked in to hotel Grand Mercure. 6th day we went to batik Winotosastro, Keraton Yogyakarta, Gereja Hati KUdus Ganjuran, and finally if not the most popular tourist attraction malioboro! The next day we checked out of the hotel and started the journey to solo. There we went to Institur Seni Indonesia-Solo and Rumah Atsiri, then checked in to hotel Novotel. Finally our final day in Jogja-solo we went to Museum Manusia Purba Sangiran - krikilan and De Tjolomadoe. Lastly we went to the airport and flew back to Jakarta.
